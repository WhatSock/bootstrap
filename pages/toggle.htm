Accessible Toggle

A toggle control is used to dynamically show or hide content.

The ARIA toggle button controls are keyboard accessible:

  • Press the Tab key to navigate between the ARIA toggle buttons.
  • Press the Spacebar to toggle the state of the selected toggle button.
  • Mouse users can click a toggle button to toggle the state as usual.
Bootstrap Syntax
Attributes

Bootstrap Syntax

<div
role="button"
class="accToggle"
data-src="path/file.html#toggleIdContent"
data-defaultopen="true"
data-insert="insertionPointElementID"
data-toggleclass="togglePressed"
id="uniqueID"
> ... </div>

Or

<div
role="button"
class="accToggle"
data-internal="toggleIdContent"
data-defaultopen="true"
data-insert="insertionPointElementID"
data-toggleclass="togglePressed"
id="uniqueID"
> ... </div>
